Gender Inequality in Tech Industry

Welcome to this website!

This website is dedicated to addressing and raising awareness about gender inequality in the Tech industry

Why Noticing Gender Inequality Matters?

Diversity in any environment fosters creativity and drives better outcomes. Research indicates that diverse communities, whether in workplace or society, tend to spark higher levels of satisfaction. Having women's voice in the team is important.

The Statistics

While women make up 47% of all employed adults in the US, as of 2022, they hold only 28% of computing roles according to Zippia. In fact, the ratio of women to men in tech roles has declined in the pasat 35 years according Accenture. Only 38% of women who majored in CS are working in the field compared to 53% of men.
